Teambuilding game with theatrical plot "In Search of Missings in the Parallel World"
According to legend, in these forests once lived a young knight in love with a poor girl. The feelings were sincere, and therefore, despite the many obstacles, they married. The girl was very afraid of losing her love and asked the Carpathian magicians to cook such a potion from which he would never stop loving her and gave them a long young life. She managed to convey to a young knight a ready drink, he got it and drank it. That girl forgot about the important detail - a drink had to be drunk in small quantities. She did not warn him about it. Her husband drunk everything until the last drop, and the time for him began to flow much slower, he did not grow up, he did not grow old and stayed for a short time in his charming temporal dimension, but the young princess continued to move faster in time So they got lost in parallel worlds. But not all is lost! You are the ones who will be able to assemble the ingredients of another beverage-countermeasure. Having complited all the parts, the teams will be able to find a place where the Knight and the Kniazhna are located.
The program is organized in the form of an active game. Teams are accompanied by specially trained instructors. Tasks and rules are formulated so that the team gets the maximum experience in interaction on the way to achieving the goal. Success depends on everyone's efforts, so the participants have to act together as "the only team".
Instructors give the team maximum freedom in work, directing and coordinating work and discussion in the group. Their task is to make people in the group discover new qualities in themselves and their teammates, create new trust relationships and realize all the benefits of working in a single team.

Bathing in thermal pools.
Complex "Thermal waters of Kosino" is located in the village. Koson, in Transcarpathia.
The territorial location of the complex near the Hungarian border significantly influenced the style in which it was executed. It is basically Austro-Hungarian style, which corresponds to white stucco and brown wood, specific carvings on it.
There are 4 thermal pools on the territory, as well as 2 large swimming pools with fresh water. The healing water of the resort rises from one of the three currently functioning, at the present time, suburbs in Transcarpathia, under the name of 16 T. The hot geyser of thermal water runs from the very heart of the earth, and then, cooled down its path. The depth of the well is 1190 meters underground. On the surface the water enters the temperature of about +55 Â° C, cooled down and fed in the pools.
