Lviv+Carpatian Mountains


1 day

Arrival to Lviv. Transfer to the hotel. Accomodation. Time for the rest.
Meeting with professional guide near your hotel.
Lviv is recognised as UNESCO World Heritage Site. 4- hours sightseeing walking tour includes: University, Lviv Opera House, The Market Square, The Town Hall. We will see Black stone house, Pharmacy Museum, a former Dominican church with catacombs, tower Kornyakta of the Assumption Church ansambel, Armenian Quarter ansambel with the Armenian cathedral. During the tour you can take photos near our town founder Daniel the King monument, will see the first Lviv tram trailer, electric trams, visit the main Roman Catholic Cathedral Latin sanctuary and within the tombs of former families of Boim and Kampians names, as well as you will see the oldest Lviv quarter – Pidzamche (Near the Castle). There are St. Nicolas Church, St. John the Baptist church and Old Market Square.
Duration – 4 hours.
Free time.

2 day

Tour guide meets you on hotel parking lot.
Transfer to village Urych.
Visiting museum and rock complex „Tustan”.
Tustan – a unique place that gives us an idea of the fortress-on-the-rock type of construction, that are hard to find in Europe. The fortress city is made of the complex of rocks named Stone, Sharp Stone, Little Rock, Groove and other rocks adjacent to the Raven mountain chain. In the IX century fortification was built there, as customs and defensive point, by the tribes of White Croats.
Visiting the Museum "Tustan." The exhibition, in addition to the history of rock fortress, tells you about the life, traditions and history of the Carpathians.
Transfer to small village „Rovin”, near village Dovge.
Baking local boyki pies “Knyshi” and cooking mushroom stew classes.
Hostess pani Slava will share recipes and cooking secrets for local boyki dishes. How to make potato dough? What is difference between mushroom soup and mushroom stew? Why highlanders eat a lot of cereal? You can get answers to these questions at the master class.
Dinner with what you’ve made :)
4 pm – 6 pm – transfer to village and walking to Sopit Waterfalls. Transfer to the hotel.

3 day

Breakfast. Check out.
Transfer to beginning of the climbing route on mountain chain Parashka.
10.00 am – 6 pm – climbing to the highest peak of national park "Skole Beskydy" m. Parashka.
Parashka Mountain is located around 10 kilometers west of Skole. It is from this mountain that you can see well the surrounding landscape. The route to the mountain starts in Skole town (the route is 10 km long and 800 m high). In addition to basic ridge tops we will climb another peak m. Zelena (1221 m) and m. Kobyla (1231 m).
Parashka Mountain (1270 m) - the highest peak of the ridge. From the mountain you can see beautiful views on all sides, and if lucky, you can even see Lviv and Stryi cities (90km).
At the top - snacks (sandwiches, fruit, nuts).
Dinner at restaurant «Viking Carpathians» at village Nyznje Synevydne. Menu: Kettle goulash, bread, herbal tea, cheesecakes.
Returning to Lviv.
Tour costs – 280 $ / per 2 persons.
